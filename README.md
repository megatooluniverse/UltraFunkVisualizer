The **Ultra Phonk Visualizer** is a powerful, browser-based, fully customizable audio-reactive visualizer designed specifically for heavy bass, Phonk, Trap, and EDM music creators. Build professional-tier music videos for YouTube or Instagram Reels directly from your browser—no heavy software like After Effects needed!

---

## 🔥 Pro-Level Features

* 🎵 **Advanced Audio-Reactive Engine:** Features a clean, symmetric frequency bar graph, glowing particle emitters, and intense screen shake/camera pumps triggered only by heavy 808 bass drops.
* 📱 **1-Click Aspect Ratio:** Seamlessly switch between **16:9** (Desktop/YouTube) and **9:16** (Mobile/Reels) formats without reloading.
* 🖼️ **Total Media Customization:** Load your own local audio/video tracks, background images, and center spinning logos. *(All processing happens locally in your browser—no data is uploaded!)*
* 🔠 **Editable Typography:** Click and type to instantly update the Song Title and Producer Name. Easily move the text to the Top or Bottom of the screen with a single click.
* 🎬 **Cinematic Aesthetics:** Includes VHS/CRT scanline overlays, dynamic pulsing vignettes, motion blur trails, and idle 3D camera drift to keep the frame feeling "alive" even during slow parts.
* 🔴 **Record Mode (Clean Screen):** Hides all UI elements and the mouse cursor for flawless screen recording. 

---

## 🛠️ How to Use (Step-by-Step)

1. **Open the Visualizer:** Go to the [Live Link](https://megatooluniverse.github.io/UltraFunkVisualizer/).
2. **Customize Media:** Use the bottom panel to upload your Background Image (🖼️) and Center Logo (🎯).
3. **Load Track:** Click **🎬 Load Video / Audio** and select your music file.
4. **Edit Details:** Click on "SONG TITLE" and "PRODUCER NAME" to type your own text.
5. **Adjust Format:** Choose your preferred layout (16:9 or 9:16) and text position.
6. **Record:** Click the **🔴 HIDE UI (REC)** button, start your screen recording software (like OBS or Windows Game Bar), and let the beat drop!
   > *💡 Pro Tip: Simply **Double-Click** anywhere on the screen to exit Record Mode and bring the UI back.*

## 💻 Tech Stack
Built completely from scratch using:
* **HTML5 & CSS3:** For responsive layout, flexbox structuring, and pure CSS glassmorphism UI.
* **Vanilla JavaScript:** For core logic, DOM manipulation, and handling media uploads.
* **Web Audio API:** For real-time frequency data extraction (`AnalyserNode`) and bass detection.
* **HTML5 Canvas:** For rendering high-performance 60FPS particles, glowing oscilloscope bars, and motion trails.
